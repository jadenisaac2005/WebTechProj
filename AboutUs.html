Our Story

The Amity University Merchandise Store is a student project created as part of the Web Technology course. Our goal is to provide a seamless and enjoyable online shopping experience for Amity students, alumni, and staff to purchase official university merchandise.

What We Offer

  • Wide range of official Amity merchandise: accessories, apparel, collectibles, gifts, and home & office items
  • Modern, responsive web design for all devices
  • Easy-to-use shopping cart and checkout experience
  • Simple login for a personalized experience
